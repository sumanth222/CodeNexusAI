Todays topic: Starting something new

Starting something new, may seem simple but is a very bold move one can make. Leaving the thing one always does and doing something new takes a lot of courage.
Just like how I have started this, there are a lot of things that could happen.
There is a chance that no one could ever use this, me and my couple of friends could be the only ones who use this. All the time resources I had spent on this could entirely go to waste.

But that is not the point of this.
By deciding to start something new itself, you are doing something that most of the people are afraid to do.
But deciding itself is not enough, hanging on to an idea even if is big or small, going through with it, no matter what, is the important step.
No matter if you win or lose, if you decide to do something new and actually accomplish it you have already won!
